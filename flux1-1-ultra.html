What is Flux 1.1 Pro Ultra?

Flux 1.1 Pro Ultra is an advanced AI image generator model that can create highly realistic images up to 4 megapixels in resolution. It offers both ultra and raw modes for maximum flexibility in image generation.

What are the key features of Flux 1.1 Pro Ultra?

The key features include the ability to generate images up to 4 megapixels in size, a raw mode optimized for photorealistic outputs, and enhanced natural image generation capabilities compared to previous models.

How can I try Flux 1.1 Pro Ultra?

What is the difference between ultra and raw modes?

While both modes can generate high-quality images, the raw mode is specifically optimized for creating highly realistic and natural-looking images. Ultra mode offers more general-purpose image generation capabilities.

Flux 1.1 Pro Ultra stands out with its ability to generate larger 4 megapixel images, enhanced realism particularly in raw mode, and improved natural image generation compared to previous versions.
